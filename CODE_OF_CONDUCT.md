# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to a respectful and professional project environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on the work.
- Assume good intent while asking for clarity.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, abuse, or personal attacks.
- Public disclosure of private security issues before a fix is available.
- Spam or intentionally disruptive participation.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block participants who violate this code of conduct.
